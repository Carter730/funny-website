Funnion's Funny Author

Many people enjoy being funny around friends and family. Carter Russ is no different he is a comedic master, has made many faces smile. Carter is a 15 year old freshman attending Licking Heights, he spends his time playing video games, singing, playing music, and attending musicals or musical performances. Carter was born into the prideful state of Ohio in it’s capital, Columbus on July 30th, 2004. His confidence in a social environment derives from participating in theater and music performances, pushing his comedic deliveries to the limit. As a freshman, Carter is deciding that he wants to major in one of three things, theater production or education, or music education.

Acting

In the spring of 2018, he took part in his first musical production, The Sound Of Music, as Kurt Vontrapp. Before that, he participated in a play called, “A Midsummer Night’s Dream,” This performance was his first ever performance in acting, kindling a flame waiting to be awoken.

Music

For Carter, music means a lot more. He officially began playing music in the year of 2013, when he began playing guitar and singing. Carter began guitar and singing lessons at a small music studio in Columbus. Now, he plays trumpet and plays in the Liking Heights Wind Ensemble and is a proud member of the Licking Heights Marching Band.
